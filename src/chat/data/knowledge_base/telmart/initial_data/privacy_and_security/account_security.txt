TelMart Account Security

Safeguarding Your TelMart Account:

If You Detect Suspicious Account Access:
- Right away, modify your password to a robust, distinctive one.
- Activate two-factor authentication when obtainable.
- Clear any stored payment details as a safeguard.
- Should you use the identical email and password across other accounts beyond TelMart.com, update those passwords simultaneously.
- Examine your Purchase History to spot any unfamiliar purchases.
- Look over your account settings for any unexpected modifications to your email, contact number, delivery address, or payment details.
- Get in touch with TelMart Customer Care by selecting the Chat with us option on TelMart.com.

Indicators of Questionable Account Activity:
- Purchases in your Purchase History that you never authorized.
- Modifications to your personal account details (email, phone, address) without your knowledge.
- Getting notification emails for activities you didn't perform.
- Obtaining a digital receipt with an unrecognized phone number or name.

Key Steps for Maintaining Account Security:
- Choose a unique password for each of your accounts. Longer passwords are tougher to crack.
- Stay away from personal details or everyday terms when creating passwords.
- Link together 3+ words to develop a unique passphrase that's meaningful only to you.
- Mix in numbers, special symbols, capital and lowercase characters.
- Security questions offer added safeguards, yet responses must not contain details readily accessible on the internet.
- Steer clear of public Wi-Fi networks when accessing your account.
- Avoid allowing browsers to save your passwords.
