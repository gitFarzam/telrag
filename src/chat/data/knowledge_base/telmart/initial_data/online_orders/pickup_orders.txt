TelMart In-Store Collection Purchases

Your purchase is prepared, TelMart will deliver a Ready for Collection communication in case you've selected to get from your neighborhood store.

Registering for Collection:
- When you reach the outlet, register by utilizing the TelMart application or by adhering to the guidelines in your Ready for Collection communication.
- A TelMart worker will carry your purchase to the assigned collection zone.

Collection Individual:
- At checkout, you may pick or modify the Collection individual.
- Pick All items, then pick Modify beside Collection individual.
- Input the Collection individual's title and contact info, then pick Keep.
- To change the Collection individual after distributing your purchase, go to Purchase Information, navigate to the purchase, and pick Modify beside Collection individual.

Changing or Changing Collection Schedule:
- You might be capable to modify your Collection location for a brief timeframe following distributing your purchase.
- You'll possess 3 hours prior to the Shipment/Collection window to modify the purchase (express purchases omitted).
- You're able to merely modify the location once per transaction.
- You're able to change the timeframe once.
- You cannot modify the fulfillment approach (like, from Collection to Shipment) following buying, excluded if you obtain a communication regarding a postponed arranged shipment that supplies this choice. To change, you need to eliminate and position a new purchase.

When You're Running Late:
- If you're delayed to your scheduled Collection, and the Collection window is prior to 8 p.m., you're still able to register via 8 p.m. neighborhood moment on the Collection date.
- In case you miss the deadline after 8 p.m., you should modify or eliminate and arrange a different transaction.
- Foods and perishable goods such as vegetables are kept at the proper temperature till you get your purchase. In case you don't gather the transaction by the closing of the date, the outlet voids your purchase the next date.
