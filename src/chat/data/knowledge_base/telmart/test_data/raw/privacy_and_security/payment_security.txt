TelMart Payment Security

Protecting Your Payment Information:

Secure Payment Practices:
- Review linked payment methods regularly by checking recent transactions in your TelMart account.
- If you suspect your payment information has been compromised, remove all saved payment details from your TelMart account immediately.
- Go to Account, then Settings gear icon, then Wallet, and select Edit to delete saved payment methods.
- Secure your account before adding new payment details.

Monitoring for Fraudulent Transactions:
- Set up transaction alerts in your banking app to detect unauthorized charges early.
- If you catch unfamiliar charges early, you can dispute them and attempt to get your money back.
- Contact your financial institutions immediately if you notice fraudulent transactions.
- Call your bank's fraud department or the number printed on the back of your card.

Authorization Holds:
- A temporary authorization hold may be placed on your payment method when placing an online order.
- Once your final total is confirmed, any leftover amount will be refunded.
- The bank will remove the hold based on their policies, which can take up to 10 days after the order is complete.
- TelMart cannot remove the hold; only the bank can.

Protection Against Shipping Scams:
- TelMart, sellers on TelMart.com, and shipping carriers will not contact you to pay any fees for delivery of your item after purchase.
- If you receive such a request, do not pay and report it to TelMart Customer Care.

If Your Payment Information Is Compromised:
- Contact your financial institution immediately and ask for the fraud department.
- Notify your credit card companies by calling the number on the back of your card.
- Consider filing a report with your local police department to document the case.
- Monitor your credit reports for unusual activity and place fraud alerts or credit freezes if needed.
