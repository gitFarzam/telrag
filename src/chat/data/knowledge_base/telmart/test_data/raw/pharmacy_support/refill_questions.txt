TelMart Pharmacy Prescription Refills

How to Refill Prescriptions Using the TelMart App:
1. Sign into your TelMart account.
2. Select Services.
3. Select Pharmacy.
4. Verify your 4-digit PIN (or use biometrics, if available).
5. Select Refill Prescriptions.
6. Select the checkbox next to any available prescription.
7. Select Continue.
8. Review the Pickup location.
9. Review the order and make changes if needed. To change the payment type, select Edit in the Payment field.
10. Select Place Order to submit your refill order.

Scan to Refill:
To use the Scan to Refill option in the TelMart app:
1. Sign into your TelMart account.
2. Select Services, then select Pharmacy.
3. Verify your 4-digit PIN (or use biometrics, if available).
4. Select Refill Prescriptions.
5. Select the Scan to Refill option and scan the barcode on your prescription bottle.

Prescription Status:
- You can check the status of your prescription refill order through Purchase History in the TelMart app or on TelMart.com.
- TelMart will notify you when your prescription is ready for pickup or delivery.

Prescription Refill Eligibility:
- Not all prescriptions are available for refill at all times. Refill eligibility depends on the prescription type and the date of the last fill.
- Contact your TelMart Pharmacy directly if you have questions about refill eligibility.

TelMart Pharmacy Contact:
- Find your pharmacy store phone number on your prescription bottle or label.
- You can also use the TelMart Store Finder on TelMart.com to locate your pharmacy and its contact information.

For questions about estimated prescription refill costs, visit the help article on Estimated Prescription Refill Costs at TelMart.com/help.
