TelMart Delivery Complaints

If you have a complaint about a TelMart delivery experience, the following resources are available:

Order Not Received:
- If your Purchase History shows your package has arrived but you cannot find it, look around the delivery location, check the delivery confirmation photo, ask your household and neighbors, and verify the address saved in your account.
- Wait two business days. It is rare, but sometimes carriers mark packages as delivered before they actually arrive.
- If you still have not received your package after two business days, use the Chat with us button on TelMart.com to contact Customer Care.

Delivery Driver Feedback:
- To provide feedback about a delivery driver (positive or negative), visit TelMart.com/help.
- A Driver Feedback and Tips option is available in the Help Center for delivery-related feedback.

Incorrect or Missing Items in Delivery:
- If items are missing from your delivery, go to your order tracking page, check the number of packages for your order, and verify if all packages have been delivered.
- If you have received all packages and are still missing items, select the Contact us button in the Help Center.

Late or Delayed Delivery:
- If there is a delay in your scheduled delivery, you may receive a text or push notification with an option to change your delivery order to a pickup order.
- If you pick up your order as a result of the delay, TelMart will refund your delivery fees and tip.
- Contact Customer Care via the Chat with us button if your order has not arrived after the delivery window has passed.

Delivery Complaint Submission:
- Visit the Corporate Feedback Form on TelMart.com and choose the relevant topic (e.g., Delivery Complaints).
- Provide as much detail as possible, including your order number, delivery date, and a description of the issue.
- Alternatively, contact Customer Care by chat, phone at 1-400-840-4060, or email at help@telmart.com.
