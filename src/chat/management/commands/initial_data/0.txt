Lumiere Beauty is a full-service online beauty retailer dedicated to providing high-quality skincare, makeup, haircare, and wellness products to customers across the United States and selected international markets. Founded in 2021 and headquartered in Los Angeles, California, the company was created with the mission of making premium beauty accessible, transparent, and convenient for everyone.

Our online store currently offers more than 2,500 products from over 75 carefully selected brands. These brands range from established global names to emerging independent labels that meet our strict quality and safety standards. Every product featured on our website is reviewed by our internal quality team to ensure it meets ingredient transparency, manufacturing reliability, and performance expectations.

Product Categories and Selection

Our catalog includes a wide variety of categories designed to meet diverse beauty needs:

Skincare: Cleansers, toners, exfoliants, serums, moisturizers, masks, eye treatments, sunscreen, and targeted treatments for acne, pigmentation, aging, and sensitivity.

Makeup: Foundations, concealers, powders, blushes, bronzers, highlighters, lipsticks, lip glosses, mascaras, eyeliners, eyeshadow palettes, brow products, and makeup tools.

Haircare: Shampoos, conditioners, hair masks, scalp treatments, styling products, heat protectants, and hair growth solutions.

Nail Care: Nail polishes, gel kits, nail strengtheners, cuticle oils, and professional manicure tools.

Fragrance and Body Care: Body lotions, scrubs, perfumes, deodorants, and wellness items.

We provide detailed product descriptions that include full ingredient lists, usage instructions, skin-type recommendations, and customer reviews to help shoppers make informed decisions.

Shipping and Delivery

We ship to all 50 U.S. states and offer international delivery to selected countries. Orders are processed within 24 business hours from Monday to Friday.

Shipping options include:

Standard shipping (3–5 business days) at $4.99
Express shipping (1–2 business days) at $12.99
Free standard shipping on orders over $50

Customers receive a tracking number via email once the order has been dispatched. Delivery timelines may vary during peak seasons, holidays, or promotional events.

Returns and Refund Policy

Customer satisfaction is a top priority. We offer a 30-day return policy from the date of delivery.

Customers may return:
Unopened and unused products in original packaging
Gently used products (no more than 20 percent used)

Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5–7 business days after the returned items are inspected and approved. If a product arrives damaged or defective, customers are encouraged to contact our support team within 48 hours and provide photos for faster resolution.

Payments and Security

We accept major credit cards, including Visa, Mastercard, and American Express, as well as PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Klarna for installment payments.

All transactions are protected through SSL encryption and secure payment gateways to ensure customer data privacy and security. We do not store full payment details on our servers.

Product Safety and Compliance

All products sold through Lumiere Beauty comply with FDA cosmetic regulations and relevant international cosmetic safety standards. Ingredient transparency is mandatory for all brands we partner with.

While our products are dermatologically tested when applicable, we recommend that customers perform a patch test before introducing a new product into their routine, especially if they have sensitive or reactive skin.
