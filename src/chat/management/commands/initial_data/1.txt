-> Menu
Our restaurant currently offers three types of burgers: hamburger, cheeseburger, and veggie burger.
All burgers are available with either a single or double patty.
Hamburger ingredients: potato bun, high-quality beef patty, pickles, special sauce
Cheeseburger ingredients: potato bun, high-quality beef patty, two layers of American cheese, pickles, special sauce
Veggie burger ingredients: gluten-free bun, chickpea-based veggie patty, pickles, special vegan sauce
