Hi! This is a demo. There is an imaginary store called 🛍️TelMart, and you are a customer chatting with a customer service AI agent. You can ask questions for which answers are already available to the AI agent, such as:

  • What is your customer service phone number?
  • What are your working hours?
  • What is your refund policy?

The AI agent will most likely be able to answer these questions using its stored knowledge and RAG system.

Later, you may ask questions that are not necessarily covered in the database, and the AI agent may not be able to answer them. For example:

  • Do you have any special discounts for 2026?
  • How can I obtain a membership card?
  • Do you have a floral section where customers can come and buy in person?

In that case, the AI agent will try to contact a human agent via Telegram. Once the human agent responds (by replying to the message), the answer will be delivered to you.
