Finding Items at TelMart

Locating Products Within the Store:
- Ask any TelMart worker in the storefront for support in discovering a product. Employees are stationed all over the storefront and have training to assist buyers find goods.
- Use the TelMart application to investigate a product and see its spot inside your neighborhood store. The application will point you toward the correct row and department.
- Look at row nameplates and section indicators everywhere in the storefront to get to the proper location.

Searching for Products on the Web:
- Go to TelMart.com or activate the TelMart application.
- Utilize the research function to look for the product by designation, company, or expression.
- Limit outcomes by department, expense, company, evaluations, and other criteria.
- Examine product availability at your neighborhood store by putting in your area code or turning on location services.

Product Availability Status:
- The supply of goods varies by storefront and web inventory.
- On the product listing on TelMart.com, you may examine whether a product is offered for in-storefront buying, in-store collection, or delivery at your neighborhood store.
- If a product is unavailable, you might have the choice to join notifications for when it gets back in supply on TelMart.com.

TelMart+ Scan and Go:
TelMart+ members have access to the Scan and Go function in the TelMart application to scan goods as you browse and buy without waiting in a payment queue.

Want support discovering a good? Go to the Customer Service Desk at your neighborhood store or phone TelMart Customer Care at 1-400-840-4060.
