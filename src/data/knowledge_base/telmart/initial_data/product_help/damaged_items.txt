TelMart Damaged Items

When You Receive a Damaged Item from TelMart.com:
- In case a product you obtained from TelMart.com arrived with damage or problems, the item might qualify for a mail return or in-store return.
- The broken item might be eligible for reimbursement or a substitute.
- Begin your return through the TelMart app or on the web by going to your Purchase History and choosing Begin a return.

Damaged Items Sold by Marketplace Sellers:
- For products purchased from a Third-party seller, reach out to the seller through the Contact seller choice found on the purchase or product listing.
- Third-party sellers handle the processing, shipping, exchanges, and buyer help for their products.
- As soon as the Marketplace Return Center processes your returned product, the reimbursement will be handled in 2 business days.

Broken Major Appliances Upon Arrival:
- Look over your purchase before you finalize the delivery, provide your signature, and before you remove it from its packaging.
- When you see any damage prior to acknowledging and putting your signature, you have the option to turn down the delivery without any fee.
- Substitute items and reimbursements for opened or broken major appliances can take place within the 2-day window from arrival.
- Over the following 28 days, exchanges and reimbursements for opened products are at the merchant's decision and may have fees.
- Mounted items cannot be exchanged; you should depend on the manufacturer's guarantee.

Marketplace Product Conditions:
- TelMart Marketplace requires merchants to specify the condition of products when creating listings.
- Products marked as New with Defects are brand-new and operational but might have exterior flaws from production or transport.

Require help with a broken item? Go to the TelMart Help Center at TelMart.com/help, choose the Chat with us option, or go to the Customer Service Desk at your nearby TelMart outlet.
