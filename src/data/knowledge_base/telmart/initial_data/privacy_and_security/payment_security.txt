TelMart Payment Security

Shielding Your Financial Details:

Safe Payment Methods:
- Frequently examine your linked payment accounts by looking at current transactions within your TelMart profile.
- If you think your payment information has been leaked, take out all stored payment methods from your TelMart account straight away.
- Navigate to Account, then click the Settings icon, then Wallet, and choose Edit to take out saved payment details.
- Make sure your account is secure prior to entering fresh payment information.

Tracking Unlawful Transactions:
- Initiate transaction warnings in your banking platform to spot questionable expenses immediately.
- When you discover unfamiliar expenses promptly, you're able to dispute them and potentially recover your cash.
- Notify your banks without delay when you discover fraudulent expenses.
- Phone your bank's fraud division or dial the number on your card's reverse side.

Authorization Holds:
- Your payment method could have a temporary authorization hold when submitting an online order.
- Once your complete amount is accepted, leftover funds will be reimbursed.
- Your financial institution will clear the hold per their timeline, which can span 10 business days following order completion.
- TelMart has no power to take away the hold; only your bank can.

Guarding Against Shipping Scams:
- TelMart, independent retailers on TelMart.com, and shipping companies will never ask you to pay additional charges for delivery after you've completed your purchase.
- Should you get such a solicitation, ignore it and inform TelMart Customer Care.

If Your Payment Information Gets Compromised:
- Get in touch with your financial institution right away and request their fraud team.
- Alert your bank card firms by dialing the number printed on your card's back.
- File a formal complaint with your regional law enforcement to have the event documented.
- Keep track of your credit reports for questionable behaviors and enable fraud notifications or account locks as necessary.
