TelMart Privacy and Personal Information

TelMart's Commitment to Confidentiality:
TelMart treasures the trust that shoppers, team members, agents, and contractors offer when they share personal details. TelMart is convinced that confidentiality goes further than just following regulations and works to handle personal data with reverence for the person.

Privacy Guidelines:
TelMart supplies Confidentiality Guidelines outlining how personal details are gathered, used, and distributed. These guidelines can be seen at corporate.telmart.com/privacy-security.

Privacy Accreditations:
As element of its extensive approach to protecting confidentiality and information security, TelMart occasionally engages in confidentiality efforts and keeps confidentiality accreditations for particular goods, offerings, and engagements. Information regarding current confidentiality accreditations can be viewed at corporate.telmart.com/privacy-security.

eReceipts and Personal Details:
Everything shared with TelMart by customers using the TelMart eReceipts function is subject to the TelMart Confidentiality Guidelines. TelMart has the right to edit, limit, or discontinue your use of TelMart eReceipts whenever at its sole discretion.

Removing Personal Information from Devices:
Before disposing of wireless devices, tablets, PCs, or comparable tech devices where you've kept personal details, erase that information fully following the producer's recommendations. For added help, check out the guidance piece on How To Clear Your Personal Information Before You Throw Out Your Phone.

Data Broker Consciousness:
Personal details posted openly on the net may be gathered by info brokers and leveraged by anybody. Limiting personal details shared online can safeguard your confidentiality.

Handling Your Personal Information in Your TelMart Account:
- Examine and modify your account details via Account, then the Settings icon on TelMart.com or the TelMart application.
- You have the ability to adjust your name, email, phone, delivery locations, and payment details whenever you like.

For more information concerning TelMart's privacy methods or to send a confidentiality request, go to corporate.telmart.com/privacy-security or reach out to TelMart Customer Care.
