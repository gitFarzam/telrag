TelMart Trade-In Approach

Products obtained from TelMart outlets or TelMart.com may be swapped within the standard return timeframe. You're able to replace most items in 90 days following buy or receipt unless otherwise noted.

Items purchased and transported by a Third-party merchant might be swapped in 30 days.

To replace an item:
- Deliver the product with your purchase slip to the Customer Service Area at any TelMart location.
- In case you obtained it via the web, you may start a replacement via your Purchase Information on TelMart.com or the TelMart app.

Products must be in fresh condition with maker packaging when relevant.

Items purchased from third-party dealers and independent retailers and not by TelMart or TelMart.com immediately are not permitted for replacement.

For devices and cell phones, reduced replacement periods are in place:
- Majority of Consumer Tech: 30 days
- Cell phones: 14 days

Third-party Marketplace Appliances:
- Replacements and reimbursements may take place in the 2-day window of arrival for exposed/put in goods.
- You should look at your acquisitions prior to signing for delivery, putting your John Hancock, and prior to unpacking the item.
- When you see any damage prior to signing and acknowledging, you have the choice to decline the shipment at no price.
- Mounted goods cannot be delivered and customers should depend on the manufacturer warranty.

For further details about substitutions, examine the TelMart Standard Return Approach or phone Client Support using the Chat with us option on TelMart.com.
