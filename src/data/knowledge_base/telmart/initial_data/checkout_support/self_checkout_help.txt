TelMart Automated Checkout Help

TelMart automated pay locations are accessible in numerous outlet places to offer a quicker checkout experience for buyers.

Utilizing Automated Pay:
- Examine every product's barcode utilizing the station scanner.
- Deposit examined goods in the bagging zone.
- Examine your buy on the display prior to proceeding to settlement.
- Utilize the Examine Your Purchase option prior to settlement to verify that every products are in your list.

Typical Automated Pay Concerns:
- Overlooked examination: If the platform does not document a product, examine it once more or press the Support or Help button to notify a neighboring worker.
- Re-examination: Examine your receipt prior to exiting the outlet to validate goods were not examined 2x.
- Barcode troubles: If a barcode is harmed or is unable to be examined, press the Support or Help button for worker aid.
- Platform troubles: In case the framework pauses or displays a mistake, do not push invoices into the apparatus or repeatedly press buttons. Press the Help or Support button on the display to notify a TelMart worker.

Automated Pay and TelMart+:
TelMart reserves a few automated pay entry for TelMart+ subscribers and shipment drivers in certain outlets to offer a more dependable, quicker encounter for frequent consumers. Modifications to automated pay configurations are executed at the outlet controller's determination per input from workers and buyers, buying habits, and organization requires in the zone.

Worker Support:
TelMart workers are educated to help with automated pay troubles, such as resolving barcode troubles, explaining product rules, and assisting with settlement. Press the Help button on screen to get immediate assistance.

In case you experience a money apparatus mistake at automated pay, keep at the station to verify your exchange is appropriately handled and press the Help or Support button.
