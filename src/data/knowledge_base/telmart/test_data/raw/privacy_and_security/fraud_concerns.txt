TelMart Fraud Alerts and Concerns

Fraud and Scam Awareness:
TelMart publishes fraud alerts at corporate.telmart.com/privacy-security to educate customers about known fraud and scams not originating from TelMart.

Common Fraud and Scam Types:
- Phishing: Fake emails or websites designed to look like TelMart to capture personal or payment information. If an offer seems too good to be true, it is likely phishing. Do not click on suspicious links, download files, or reveal sensitive information over the phone.
- Account Takeovers: Fraudsters may gain unauthorized access to your TelMart account to make purchases, change account details, or exploit saved payment information.
- Identity Theft: Unauthorized accounts or suspicious purchase attempts may indicate identity theft.
- Fake Customer Service Representatives: Scammers may call or contact you claiming to be TelMart representatives. Verify that you are contacting TelMart through official channels.

Steps to Take if You Suspect Fraud:
1. Change your TelMart account password immediately.
2. Remove all saved payment information from your account.
3. Contact your financial institutions and ask for the fraud department.
4. Notify your credit card companies.
5. File a report with your local police department.
6. Contact the Social Security Administration and credit bureaus if you suspect identity theft.
7. Monitor your credit reports for unusual activity and place fraud alerts or credit freezes if needed.
8. Contact TelMart Customer Care via the Chat with us button on TelMart.com to report the fraud.

Protecting Against Fraud:
- Use strong, unique passwords for your TelMart account and all online accounts.
- Enable two-factor authentication where available.
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi when logging into your account or making purchases.
- Do not save card details in your TelMart account if you are concerned about security.
- Monitor your account and order history regularly for any unexpected activity.
- Report any suspicious emails, calls, or activity to TelMart Customer Care.

Reporting Fraud:
- TelMart Customer Care: Use the Chat with us button on TelMart.com or call 1-400-840-4060.
- Federal Trade Commission (FTC): Visit reportfraud.ftc.gov to report fraud or scams.
