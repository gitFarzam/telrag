TelMart Privacy and Personal Information

TelMart's Privacy Commitment:
TelMart values the trust that customers, associates, representatives, and service providers place in TelMart when they provide personal information. TelMart believes that privacy is more than an issue of compliance and endeavors to manage personal information in accordance with its core value of respect for the individual.

Privacy Notices:
TelMart provides Privacy Notices that explain how personal information is collected, used, and shared. These notices are available at corporate.telmart.com/privacy-security.

Privacy Certifications:
As part of its overall approach to privacy and security, TelMart occasionally participates in privacy-related programs and maintains privacy-related certifications on certain products, services, and experiences. Information about current privacy certifications is available at corporate.telmart.com/privacy-security.

eReceipts and Personal Information:
All information provided to TelMart by users of the TelMart eReceipts service is subject to the TelMart Privacy Policy. TelMart may alter, suspend, or terminate your use of TelMart eReceipts at any time at its discretion.

Removing Personal Information from Devices:
Before returning wireless phones, tablets, laptops, or other electronic devices on which you have saved personal information, erase this information completely by following the manufacturer's instructions. For further help, visit the help article on How To Remove Your Personal Information Before You Get Rid of Your Phone.

Data Broker Awareness:
Information shared publicly online can be collected by data broker websites and used by others. Limiting personal information shared online helps protect your privacy.

Managing Your Personal Information in Your TelMart Account:
- Review and update your account information by going to Account, then the Settings gear icon on TelMart.com or the TelMart app.
- You can update your name, email address, phone number, shipping addresses, and payment methods at any time.

For questions about TelMart's privacy practices or to submit a privacy-related request, visit corporate.telmart.com/privacy-security or contact TelMart Customer Care.
