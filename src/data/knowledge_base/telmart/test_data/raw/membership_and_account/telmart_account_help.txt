TelMart Account Help

Creating an Account:
- Visit TelMart.com or open the TelMart app.
- Select Sign in or create account.
- Follow the prompts to create a new account with your email address and a password.
- You can also use Passkeys as a sign-in method for added security.

Managing Your Account:
- Sign in to your TelMart account to manage your personal information, payment methods, order history, and preferences.
- To edit your account information, select Account, then the Settings gear icon.

Purchase History:
- Sign into your account and select Purchase History to view past orders, track shipments, and start returns.

Payment Methods:
- Add, edit, or remove payment methods by going to Account, then the Settings gear icon, then Wallet.
- TelMart accepts credit cards, debit cards, PayPal, gift cards, HSA/FSA cards, and bank accounts.

Account Security:
- TelMart recommends using a strong, unique password for your account.
- If you suspect unauthorized activity on your account, change your password immediately and contact Customer Care.
- Enable two-factor authentication if available.

Tax-Exempt Purchases:
- If you are a tax-exempt organization, visit TelMart.com/help for information on setting up a tax-exempt account.

Pharmacy Access:
- To add pharmacy access to your account, visit the Pharmacy section in the app or on TelMart.com and follow the identity verification steps.

For account-related issues, visit TelMart.com/help or contact Customer Care via the Chat with us button, phone at 1-400-840-4060, or email at help@telmart.com.
