TelMart Warranty Information

Manufacturer Warranties:
- Products sold by TelMart may come with a manufacturer's warranty.
- The manufacturer warranty covers when the item itself is defective.
- Warranty terms and duration vary by manufacturer and product type.
- For additional information about a product's warranty, contact the manufacturer directly.
- TelMart's terms of use do not limit the terms of any product warranty offered by the manufacturer of an item sold by TelMart.

TelMart Protection Plans by Allstate:
- TelMart Protection Plans by Allstate are available for some products sold on TelMart.com.
- You can select a protection plan on the item page or at checkout for eligible items.
- Protection plans for online orders must be purchased during checkout.
- If you would like a protection plan after checkout, you can buy one in-store within 30 days of your online order with your receipt.
- Coverage starts on the date of purchase and not after the manufacturer's warranty expires.
- Standard Plan: For products such as TVs, home appliances, lawn and garden, power tools, etc.
- Accident Plan: For select portable electronics such as laptops, tablets, phones, and headphones.
- To file a claim, visit telmartprotection.com, call 1-877-538-4389, or text an image of your receipt to 202202.
- If the item cannot be repaired, TelMart will send a replacement or reimburse you.

Tire Warranty:
- TelMart offers a Limited Warranty on tires purchased and installed at TelMart Auto Care Centers.
- When making a claim, you must present your tires and the vehicle to your local TelMart Auto Care Center for inspection.
- You must also present your original invoice and TelMart installation record.
- To find the nearest TelMart that can process the replacement request, visit TelMart.com or call 800-925-6278.
- An optional Road Hazard Warranty is available for additional coverage against tire failures caused by road hazards such as nails, glass, or metal stones.

Marketplace and Resold Items Warranty:
- For third-party sellers, use the Contact seller option on the item's page to request a copy of the warranty.
- Restored (refurbished) items: Warranty information can be found on the product page.
- Premium Marketplace items: Minimum one-year written warranty required.
- Like New Marketplace items: At least 90-day free returns.
