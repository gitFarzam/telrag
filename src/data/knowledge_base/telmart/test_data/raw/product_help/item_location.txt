Finding Items at TelMart

In-Store Item Location:
- Ask any TelMart associate in the store for help finding an item. Associates are stationed throughout the store and are trained to help customers locate products.
- Use the TelMart app to search for an item and see its location within your local store. The app can guide you to the correct aisle and section.
- Look for aisle signs and department markers throughout the store to navigate to the correct section.

Online Item Search:
- Visit TelMart.com or open the TelMart app.
- Use the search bar to search for the item by name, brand, or keyword.
- Filter results by department, price, brand, ratings, and other attributes.
- Check item availability at your local store by entering your zip code or enabling location services.

Item Availability:
- Product availability varies by store location and online inventory.
- On the item page on TelMart.com, you can check if an item is available for in-store purchase, pickup, or delivery at your local store.
- If an item is out of stock, you may be able to sign up for in-stock notifications on TelMart.com.

TelMart+ Scan and Go:
TelMart+ members can use the Scan and Go feature in the TelMart app to scan items as they shop and pay without waiting in a checkout line.

If you need help locating an item, visit the Customer Service Desk at your local TelMart store or contact TelMart Customer Care at 1-400-840-4060.
