TelMart Price Match and Price Adjustment Policy

TelMart offers Price Match policies that differ between TelMart stores and TelMart.com. Details on how TelMart matches prices are available on the corporate TelMart Policies and Guidelines page.

Price and Listing Errors:
TelMart reserves the right to cancel orders placed based on pricing errors or other listing errors. Information about cancellations due to price and other listing errors is available at TelMart.com.

Temporary Holds and Charges:
When your payment method includes a prepaid or EBT card, an order adjustment debit is charged to make sure there are enough funds to complete your purchase. Once your final total is confirmed, you will be refunded any amount that is left over.

Charges and Authorization Holds for Online Orders:
A temporary authorization hold may be placed on your payment method when placing an online order. This charge includes adjustments to your final order total for items that are priced by weight, canceled, or potentially substituted. After confirming your final order total, any leftover amount will be refunded.

Once the order is complete, the bank will remove the hold based on their policies. This can take up to 10 days after the order is complete to remove the hold. TelMart cannot remove the hold; only the bank can. Please contact your bank with any other questions.

All TelMart policies applicable to sales occurring at a store location, including those regarding Ad Match, Price Matching, Coupon use, Returns, and Exchanges, apply to purchases made through TelMart Pay.
