TelMart Refund Policy

TelMart refunds TelMart.com returns submitted in-store or by mail to your original method of payment. Outbound shipping charges are not always refunded upon return. This can include regular and freight shipping, and any shipping surcharges.

Refund Timelines by Payment Method:
- Credit Card: Up to 10 business days, depending on your financial institution.
- Debit Card: Debit and credit card refunds will be available in up to 10 business days. If your purchase was made using a debit or credit card, TelMart will issue the refund to that same card.
- PayPal: Up to 10 business days, can be longer if linked to an international bank.
- EBT/Gift Card/Refund Credit: Should be refunded within three hours.
- Check: Up to 30 business days depending on your financial institution.

If the original card is not present and is not available by scanning the receipt, the refund is processed onto a TelMart shopping card or gift card.

If your purchase was a debit transaction, TelMart will place the refund on the debit card. If you do not have your receipt, show a valid government-issued photo identification.

If you cancel your order before your item ships, TelMart releases the authorization hold on your payment method. If the item has already shipped, TelMart is not able to cancel the order. You can return the item for a refund.

Marketplace Returns Refund Process:
- Once the Marketplace Return Center receives your return item, TelMart will process the refund within two business days.
- TelMart will process the refund when the carrier scans the return package.
- Please allow up to two business days after dropping off your return at a store for tracking to be available.
- Some Marketplace sellers may have a possible restocking fee (up to 20%).
- For items greater than $600, it is recommended to insure the item prior to return shipping.

Refunds issued for certain scenarios are subject to recharge if TelMart or the seller can verify delivery or frequent abuse.

Items that require age verification via a valid government-issued identification, such as alcohol, may have a refund delay of up to ten business days if TelMart is unable to verify age.

Express Bill Payments and fees associated with TelMart Financial Services products are non-refundable.
