TelMart Employee Feedback

TelMart welcomes feedback about associate interactions, whether positive or negative.

How to Submit Employee or Associate Feedback:
1. Visit the Corporate Feedback Form on TelMart.com.
2. Choose Store Experience from the Topic list (for in-store associate feedback) or the relevant topic for other feedback types.
3. Select Continue.
4. Provide details about your interaction in the Enter your Feedback section. Include the associate's name if known.
5. Enter your contact information under Tell us a little about yourself.
6. Select Continue.
7. Choose the store location associated with your feedback.

Alternative Contact Options:
- Chat with us: Available on TelMart.com via the Help Center.
- Phone: 1-400-840-4060
- Email: help@telmart.com
- In-store: Request to speak with a store manager.
- Social Media: @TelMartHelp on Twitter/X is available for customers who post about their experiences.

Positive Feedback:
If a TelMart associate provided exceptional service, TelMart encourages customers to share that feedback through the Corporate Feedback Form or by speaking with the store manager. Recognizing associates for great service helps TelMart celebrate their teams.

Incident Reports:
If you were involved in a safety incident in a TelMart store, ask to speak with a manager and request a copy of the incident report. Document all details including the date, time, store location, and a description of what occurred.
