TelMart Delivery Orders

TelMart offers delivery of groceries and other items directly to your door through local, third-party delivery drivers.

How Delivery Works:
- After the driver picks up your order, you will get a notification letting you know the driver is on their way.
- In some cases, the delivery can take up to two hours to get to you.
- If you select "I'll sign for my order," this will require you to provide a pin or signature when the driver delivers your order.
- A pin may also be required for certain orders or high-value items. You will be notified at checkout if this is required.
- Once the order is out for delivery, the four-digit passcode will show under the tracking status bar under Order Details. You can also view the pin when selecting Track Order.
- If your order is being delivered to someone else, you will need to provide the recipient with the pin for a successful delivery.

Changing Delivery Details:
- You may be able to change your Delivery address for a brief time after you place your order.
- You will have up to three hours before the Delivery window to update the order (express orders excluded).
- You can only change the address once per order.
- If you try to change to an address outside of the Delivery area, you will see a message that the order cannot be delivered to that address.

Guided Delivery Instructions:
You can add delivery instructions to your order to help drivers locate your address correctly. Be sure your Rooftop Pin is correctly set in your account for accurate delivery.

Delivery Delays:
If there is a delay in your scheduled delivery, you may get a text message or push notification inviting you to change your delivery order to a pickup order. You can choose a pickup time slot within the next 24 hours. If you pick up your order, TelMart will refund your delivery fees and tip.

Drone Delivery:
TelMart offers drone delivery in select areas. Availability may vary by location.

Undeliverable Orders:
If your order cannot be delivered, TelMart will provide options for what to do next. Contact customer care via the Chat with us button on TelMart.com if you need assistance.

For InHome Delivery (TelMart+ InHome members):
InHome associates deliver directly inside your home or garage. You will receive a notification before the associate arrives. If there is a delay in your InHome delivery, you may receive an email, text message, or push notification with options to update your order.
