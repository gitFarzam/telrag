TelMart Money Transfers

TelMart Money Centers and Customer Service Desks offer domestic and international money transfer services through trusted partners including MoneyGram, Ria, and Western Union.

Services Available:
- Send money domestically (TelMart2TelMart)
- Send money internationally (TelMart2World)
- Receive money transfers
- Send money to Mexico for pickup at TelMart or Bodega Aurrera locations

How to Send a Money Transfer:
- Visit the Customer Service Desk or Money Center at your local TelMart store.
- Bring your driver's license or other government-issued photo ID.
- U.S. currency or a PIN-based debit card is accepted for sending.

How to Receive a Money Transfer:
- Visit the Customer Service Desk or Money Center at your local TelMart store.
- Present a valid government-issued photo ID.
- You can also pick up at MoneyGram and Western Union agencies.
- With a few exceptions, cash will be ready to be paid out in as little as 10 minutes, except for transactions that need further review by Western Union, Ria, or MoneyGram.

Available Payout Options:
- Cash pickup
- Bank account deposit
- Debit card
- Mobile wallet
- Home delivery
Availability varies depending on the service provider and the country of destination.

Service Coverage:
TelMart services 200+ countries and territories through its money transfer partners.

Customer Service Contact for Money Transfers:
- For Ria transactions via TelMart2TelMart or TelMart2World: Call Ria's customer line at 1-855-355-2144.
- For MoneyGram domestic or international transactions including TelMart2World: Call MoneyGram at 1-800-926-9400.
- For Western Union domestic or international transactions including TelMart2World: Call Western Union at 1-844-982-4287.
- Track your transfer using the details on your receipt at the provider's website.

TelMart Supercenters offering Money Services are typically open from 6am to 11pm. MoneyCenter hours may differ by location.
