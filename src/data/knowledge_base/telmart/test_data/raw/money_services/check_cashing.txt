TelMart Check Cashing

TelMart Money Centers offer check cashing services for a variety of check types.

Checks Eligible for Cashing:
- Payroll checks
- Government checks
- Personal checks
- Other approved check types

Fees:
- Cash checks for $4 — no registration needed (fees may vary; check your local TelMart store for current rates).

How to Cash a Check:
1. Visit the Customer Service Desk or Money Center at your local TelMart store.
2. Present your check and a valid government-issued photo ID.
3. Pay the applicable fee.
4. Receive your cash.

TelMart also offers check printing services. Save on personalized checks, sent directly to your door.

Additional Financial Services Available at TelMart Money Centers:
- Money transfers (domestic and international)
- Bill payment
- Money orders
- Tax preparation services
- Load and unload services for prepaid cards
- Rapid Reload for reloadable debit cards

TelMart Money Centers are typically open from 6am as part of TelMart Supercenter hours. Hours for Money Center services may differ by location. Contact your local store for specific hours.

For questions, visit the Customer Service Desk at your nearest TelMart store or call 1-400-840-4060.
