TelMart Bill Payment Services

TelMart offers in-store bill payment services at Customer Service Desks and TelMart Money Centers. This service allows customers to pay bills quickly and conveniently using cash.

How to Pay Bills at TelMart:
1. Visit the Customer Service Desk or TelMart Money Center at your local store.
2. Bring a paper copy of your bill or have the barcode available on your mobile device.
3. A TelMart associate will scan the barcode tied to your account.
4. Complete your payment with cash.
5. Funds are transferred electronically through a consolidated settlement.

This service is available at all TelMart stores in the U.S.

Bill Payment Service Details:
- The cash payment process is quick and convenient.
- Consumers can pay bills and fund a variety of account types at TelMart.
- This service is designed to serve customers who prefer to pay bills with cash, including un- and under-banked customers.

TelMart also provides online bill payment options through its Money Center partners. Visit TelMart.com/money-services or the TelMart app for more details on available digital payment options.

Additional Financial Services Available at TelMart Money Centers:
- Money transfers (domestic and international)
- Check cashing
- Money orders
- Tax preparation
- Prepaid card reload

TelMart Financial Services products: Express Bill Payments and fees associated with TelMart Financial Services products are non-refundable.
