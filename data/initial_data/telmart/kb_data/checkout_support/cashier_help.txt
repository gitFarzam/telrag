TelMart Cashier Checkout Assistance

TelMart associates are available at staffed checkout lanes throughout the store to assist customers with their purchases.

Check Out With Me Service:
TelMart associates can check out customers directly on the sales floor using cellular devices and Bluetooth printers. Associates outfitted with mobile checkout devices can complete your purchase and provide a receipt (paper or electronic) wherever you are in the store. This service is positioned in the busiest areas of the store, such as the garden center, electronics, or action alley.

To complete a purchase at a staffed register:
- Place your items on the conveyor belt or hand them to the cashier.
- The cashier will scan each item and provide a running total.
- Once all items are scanned, select your payment method.
- Payment types accepted include cash, credit card, debit card, TelMart Pay, EBT/SNAP, gift cards, and other approved payment methods.

For transactions using TelMart Pay:
- Scan the on-screen QR code to connect your account.
- All TelMart policies applicable to store sales, including those regarding Ad Match, Price Matching, Coupon use, Returns, and Exchanges, apply to purchases made through TelMart Pay.

If you experience any issues at the cashier lane, ask the associate for assistance or contact the Customer Service Desk.
