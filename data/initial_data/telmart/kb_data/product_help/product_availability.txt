TelMart Product Availability

Checking Product Availability:
- Visit TelMart.com or the TelMart app and search for the item.
- On the item's product page, enter your zip code or allow location access to check in-store availability at your local store.
- The page will indicate whether the item is available for in-store purchase, pickup, or delivery.

Online vs. In-Store Availability:
- Not all products available on TelMart.com are available in every store.
- Not all in-store products are listed on TelMart.com.
- Availability can vary by store location and region.

Out-of-Stock Items:
- If an item is out of stock, check the product page for restock notifications or alternative options.
- For online orders, if an ordered item becomes unavailable before shipment, TelMart will notify you and may offer a substitution or cancel the item from your order.

Marketplace Items:
- TelMart.com also features products from third-party Marketplace sellers.
- Marketplace items are sold and shipped by third-party sellers and are not always available in TelMart stores.
- Marketplace items sold by a Marketplace seller may be returned within 30 days.

TelMart's Pricing Policy:
- TelMart uses automated pricing rules to identify and remove offers from TelMart.com that are priced unfairly or are uncompetitive.
- Market conditions change frequently. Prices are subject to change.

For specific product inquiries, visit TelMart.com, use the TelMart app, or contact your local TelMart store.
