TelMart Damaged Items

Receiving a Damaged Item from TelMart.com:
- If an item you received from TelMart.com is damaged or defective, your item may be eligible to be returned by mail or returned to any TelMart store.
- The damaged item may qualify for a refund or replacement.
- Start your return in the TelMart app or online by going to your Purchase History and selecting Start a return.

Damaged Items from Marketplace Sellers:
- For items sold by a Marketplace seller, contact the seller directly using the Contact seller link on the order or item page.
- Marketplace sellers are responsible for processing, shipping, returns, and customer service related to their orders.
- Once the Marketplace Return Center receives your return item, the refund will be processed within two business days.

Damaged Major Appliances at Delivery:
- Inspect your items before accepting delivery, signing for it, and before opening the item.
- If you notice any damage before accepting and signing, the delivery can be refused at no charge.
- Exchanges and refunds for opened or damaged major appliances can occur within the 2-day timeframe of delivery.
- For the remaining 28 days, returns and refunds for opened items are at the seller's discretion and may be subject to additional fees.
- Installed items cannot be returned; customers should defer to the manufacturer warranty.

Marketplace Item Conditions:
- TelMart Marketplace requires sellers to list the condition of items when creating a listing.
- New with Defects items are brand new and functional but may have cosmetic imperfections caused during production or delivery.

For assistance with a damaged item, visit the TelMart Help Center at TelMart.com/help, use the Chat with us button, or visit the Customer Service Desk at your local TelMart store.
