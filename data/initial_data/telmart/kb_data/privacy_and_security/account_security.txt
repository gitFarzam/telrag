TelMart Account Security

Protecting Your TelMart Account:

If You Suspect Unauthorized Account Access:
- Change your password immediately using a strong, unique password.
- Enable two-factor authentication if available.
- Delete any saved payment information as a precaution.
- If you use the same email address and password for accounts outside of TelMart.com, change your passwords for those accounts as well.
- Review your Purchase History for any orders you did not place.
- Review your account for any changes to your email, phone number, shipping address, or payment methods.
- Contact TelMart Customer Care using the Chat with us button on TelMart.com.

Signs of Unauthorized Account Activity:
- Orders you did not place appearing in your Purchase History.
- Changes to your account information (email, phone, shipping address) that you did not make.
- Receiving emails about activity you did not initiate.
- Receiving a digital receipt sent to a phone number or name that is not yours.

Best Practices for Account Security:
- Use a different password for every account. The longer the password, the more difficult it is to hack.
- Never include personal information or common words and phrases in your password.
- Combine 3 or more words together to create a phrase meaningful only to you.
- Include numbers, symbols, and both uppercase and lowercase letters.
- Security questions add a layer of protection, but answers should not be information easily found online.
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for account login.
- Do not save passwords in browsers.

For assistance with account security issues, contact TelMart Customer Care via the Chat with us button on TelMart.com, phone at 1-400-840-4060, or email at help@telmart.com.
