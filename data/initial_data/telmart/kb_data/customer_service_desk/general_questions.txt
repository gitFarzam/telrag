TelMart Customer Service - General Questions

TelMart Customer Service is available to help with a wide range of questions and concerns related to your shopping experience, both in-store and online.

Contact Options:
- Chat with us: Available on TelMart.com via the Help Center. Select the Chat with us button for live assistance.
- Phone: 1-400-840-4060
- Email: help@telmart.com
- In-store: Visit the Customer Service Desk at your local TelMart store.

Help Center:
Visit TelMart.com/help to find answers to common questions, use the online chat, and browse help topics including:
- Order tracking and management
- Returns and refunds
- Pickup and delivery
- Account management
- Pharmacy services
- TelMart+ membership
- Money services
- Privacy and security

Store Locations:
Use the Store Finder on TelMart.com to locate your nearest TelMart store, check store hours, and find available services.

TelMart Policies and Guidelines:
TelMart's most frequently requested public policies and guidelines, including the store return policy, ad match guarantee, and coupon policy, are available at TelMart.com/help under Policies.

For purchases made in-store, bring your receipt to the Customer Service Desk for assistance with returns, exchanges, price adjustments, and other inquiries.

For online orders, most issues can be managed through your Purchase History in your TelMart account on TelMart.com or the TelMart app.
