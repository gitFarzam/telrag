TelMart Store Experience Feedback

TelMart values customer feedback about in-store experiences and wants to hear from customers about their visits.

How to Submit Store Experience Feedback:
1. Visit the Corporate Feedback Form on TelMart.com.
2. Choose Store Experience from the Topic list.
3. Select Continue.
4. Give as much detail as you would like in the Enter your Feedback section.
5. Enter the required contact information under Tell us a little about yourself section.
6. Select Continue.
7. Choose the store that your feedback is for. If you cannot find the store, you can search for it by zip code, address, or contact Customer Care using the Chat with us button.

Contact Options for Store Feedback:
- TelMart.com Help Center: Visit TelMart.com/help and use the Chat with us button.
- Phone: 1-400-840-4060
- Email: help@telmart.com
- In-store: Speak with a store manager or Customer Service Desk associate directly.
- Social Media: @TelMartHelp on Twitter/X can assist with issues. You may be asked to send a direct message with details about your experience.

What to Include in Your Feedback:
- The specific store location (address or store number)
- Date and time of your visit
- A description of your experience
- Names of associates involved if relevant
- Any supporting information such as receipts or order numbers

TelMart takes store experience feedback seriously and uses it to improve the in-store experience for customers and associates.
