TelMart Online Order Status

Tracking Your Order:
- Sign into your TelMart account at TelMart.com or on the TelMart app.
- Select Account, then Purchase History.
- Find your order and select it to view the current status and tracking details.
- The order tracker displays estimated delivery dates and any updates.

Delayed Orders:
TelMart does its best to keep up with growing demand for popular items. Sometimes, pre-transit order delays still happen. If an order is delayed, TelMart will send order update emails in real-time.

Contact customer care after you have:
- Waited past the estimated delivery window.

If you are experiencing an extended delay, contact customer care using the Chat with us button on TelMart.com.

Checking for Carrier Delays:
- FedEx delays: Visit FedEx Service Alerts at fedex.com
- USPS delays: Visit USPS Service Alerts at usps.com
- UPS delays: Visit UPS Service Alerts at ups.com

Delivery Delay Notification:
If there is a delay in your scheduled delivery, you may get a text message or push notification inviting you to change your delivery order to a pickup order. You can choose a pickup time slot within the next 24 hours. If you pick up your order, TelMart will refund your delivery fees and tip.

If your driver returns your delivery to the store, an associate will contact you to reschedule your delivery. If the delivery cannot be rescheduled, TelMart will cancel the delivery order and work to resolve the issue.

For any concerns about your order status, contact customer care via the Chat with us button on TelMart.com or call 1-400-840-4060.
