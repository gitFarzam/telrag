TelMart Pharmacy Vaccine Appointments

TelMart Pharmacies offer a variety of immunizations, including flu shots, COVID-19 vaccines, and more.

Available Vaccines:
- COVID-19 vaccine (updated)
- Flu shot
- Tetanus (everyone needs an initial dose and booster every 10 years)
- Shingles vaccine (for immunocompromised adults 19+ and adults 50+)
- Additional select vaccines available at TelMart pharmacies
- Not all vaccinations are available for scheduling online.

Cost:
- $0 copay with most insurances. State, age, and health restrictions may apply.

How to Schedule a Vaccine Appointment Online:
1. Visit TelMart.com/pharmacy or the TelMart app and select Vaccines.
2. Select a vaccine type and select Continue (e.g., Shingles).
3. Select Find an appointment.
4. Or select Add another patient to schedule multiple people (up to four people under one appointment).
5. Enter a zip code (or city, state) and select Find.
6. Select a store location from the list and select Continue.
7. Select a date and time and select Continue.
8. Enter the patient information and select Continue.
9. Answer questions in the questionnaire and select Continue.
10. Acknowledge patient consent and select Continue.
11. Select Confirm Appointment.

Walk-In Availability:
You may also stop in at your convenience when the pharmacy is open for immediate vaccine assistance.

How to Cancel a Vaccine Appointment Online:
1. Open the original email confirmation from when you first booked your appointment online.
2. Search your email inbox for "Your TelMart pharmacy appointment."
3. Select the Cancel online link in the email.
4. Sign in to your TelMart.com account.
5. Verify your date of birth.

If you need to cancel by phone, contact your local pharmacy. Find the pharmacy store phone number on your prescription bottle, label, or through the TelMart Store Finder on TelMart.com.

Download Vaccine History:
You can download your vaccine history through your TelMart account. Visit TelMart.com/help for instructions on downloading vaccine history.
