TelMart Pharmacy Insurance Information

Accepted Insurance:
TelMart accepts most prescription insurance plans. Check with your insurance provider for specific coverage details.

Adding or Updating Insurance Information:
- You may update your insurance during any online refill or transfer request. Select Add or Change insurance before submitting and follow the steps.
- To update insurance by phone or add coupon codes or discount cards, call or visit your local TelMart Pharmacy.

Insurance and Discount Cards:
- You may add discount cards and coupons by calling or visiting the pharmacy.
- If you are in need of insurance, TelMart Insurance Services can help you find a plan that works for you. Visit the TelMart Insurance Services page at TelMart.com for more details.

Medicare and Medicaid:
- TelMart.com provides information on Medicare Insurance Plan options.
- For Medicaid Renewal assistance, visit the Medicaid Renewal page in the Help Center on TelMart.com.
- View Medicare Insurance Plan options at TelMart.com/help.

Vision Insurance:
- TelMart Vision Centers accept many vision insurance plans.
- Visit the Vision Benefits page at TelMart.com/help for more details.
- Use the Find a TelMart Vision Center tool to locate a Vision Center near you.

HIPAA:
- TelMart Pharmacy and Vision Center/Optical HIPAA forms are available at TelMart.com/help.

For specific insurance questions, contact your local TelMart Pharmacy directly. Find the pharmacy phone number on your prescription bottle or use the Store Finder at TelMart.com to locate your pharmacy.
