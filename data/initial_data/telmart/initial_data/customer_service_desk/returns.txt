TelMart Standard Return Policy

You have 90 days after purchase or upon receipt to return most items unless noted in our exceptions. Items sold and shipped by a Marketplace seller may be returned within 30 days.

Returns are available for nearly everything TelMart sells. Your purchase history on TelMart.com displays the latest eligible date for a return or replacement. We recommend keeping track of all manufacturer packaging and your receipt for a minimum of 90 days after purchase.

TelMart's policy applies to returns of products purchased in TelMart U.S. stores, TelMart.com, TelMart Business, or from Marketplace sellers on TelMart.com. Items purchased from dealers or resellers and not TelMart or TelMart.com directly are not eligible for return, refund, or exchange.

Most Consumer Electronics are returnable within 30 days. Wireless phones are returnable within 14 days.

Most items purchased in-store or online from October 1 through December 31 are returnable until January 31. Marketplace seller participation varies.

If an item you received from TelMart.com is damaged or defective, your item may be eligible to be returned by mail or returned to any TelMart store and may qualify for a refund or replacement. Please start your return in the TelMart app or online by going to your Purchase History.

How to Start an Online Return:
1. Select Purchase History in your account.
2. Select View details for the item you want to return.
3. Select Start a return.
4. Place a checkmark next to the item(s) you wish to return and select Continue.
5. Select the reason you'd like to return; select Next.
6. Select if you would like a refund or a replacement of the item and select Next.
7. Add comments (optional) and select Save, then select Continue.
8. Select the method you'd like to use for return, select Continue.
9. Select Submit if everything is correct.

Return Methods:
- Drop off at FedEx location: Print the return label displayed on the screen, attach it to your package, and drop your return off at FedEx.
- Drop off at a TelMart store.
- InHome Returns Pick-Up (TelMart+ InHome members): A TelMart associate will pick up the return the next day.

TelMart+ InHome members can start a return and have items picked up by a TelMart associate the next day. The associate will bring your item back to the store for a refund. InHome pickup returns are not available for items sold by Marketplace sellers or larger items delivered by Freight carriers, or grocery items.

Items Not Eligible for Return:
- Gas Powered Recreational Vehicles (e.g., Dirt Bikes, Mini-Bikes, Go-Carts, Scooters, Ride-ons, UTVs and ATVs)
- Home Diagnostic Testing Kits (including COVID-19 tests)
- Hygienic Medical Equipment (e.g., bedpans, bath seats, eye drop guides, opened/unsealed breast pumps, sitz baths)
- Items containing pseudoephedrine, ephedrine, phenylpropanolamine
- Electronic games and software downloads after purchase (unless specified)

Express Bill Payments and fees associated with TelMart Financial Services products are non-refundable.
