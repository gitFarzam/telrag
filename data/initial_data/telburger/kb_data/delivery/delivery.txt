At TelBurger, customer convenience and satisfaction are at the center of everything we do. We proudly provide multiple service options to ensure every customer can enjoy our food in the way that best fits their lifestyle, schedule, and preferences. Whether customers want to relax and enjoy their meal inside the restaurant, quickly grab food on the go, or receive their order directly at home, TelBurger is committed to delivering a smooth and enjoyable experience.

We currently offer 3 main service options for our customers:

1. Dining inside our lobby
2. Using our drive-thru service
3. Picking up orders at the counter

Our dining lobby is designed to provide a comfortable, clean, and welcoming environment for individuals, families, and groups of friends. Customers who choose to dine inside can enjoy a relaxed atmosphere while eating freshly prepared burgers, sandwiches, fries, drinks, and other menu items. The lobby is open daily from 10:00 AM until 9:00 PM, giving customers plenty of time throughout the day to visit and enjoy their meals.

For customers who prefer a faster and more convenient option, TelBurger also provides a drive-thru service. This option allows customers to place and receive their orders without leaving their vehicles, making it ideal for busy schedules, quick lunch breaks, families with children, or customers traveling on the road. Our drive-thru service operates daily from 10:00 AM until 11:00 PM, offering extended hours to better serve late-evening customers.

In addition to dine-in and drive-thru services, TelBurger supports convenient order pickup at the counter inside the lobby. Customers can place their orders ahead of time and simply arrive at the restaurant to pick them up. This option helps reduce waiting time and provides a fast and efficient experience for customers who want to enjoy TelBurger meals at home, at work, or while traveling.

TelBurger also offers delivery services directly to customers addresses through several popular food delivery platforms. Customers can conveniently order from their favorite delivery applications, including Uber Eats, DoorDash, and Grubhub. Through these platforms, customers can browse the menu, place orders online, track deliveries in real time, and enjoy TelBurger meals from the comfort of their homes or offices.

At TelBurger, we continuously work to make our services accessible, flexible, and customer-friendly. By combining dine-in experiences, convenient pickup options, efficient drive-thru operations, and reliable delivery partnerships, we aim to provide high-quality service for every type of customer.
