TelMart Support - Frequently Asked Questions

TelMart Support is ready to help with a broad spectrum of inquiries and issues linked to your buying journey, both at store and web.

Ways to Communicate:
- Chat with us: Obtainable on TelMart.com using the Support Center. Pick the Chat with us option for real-time help.
- By phone: 1-400-840-4060
- E-mail: help@telmart.com
- At a store: Go to the Service Counter at your neighborhood TelMart outlet.

Support Center:
Visit TelMart.com/help to locate responses to regular questions, utilize online assistance, and examine support resources including:
- Tracking orders and handling acquisitions
- Replacements and reimbursements
- Collection and shipment
- User account administration
- Drug services
- TelMart+ program
- Financial services
- Confidentiality and information security

Store Listings:
Utilize the Location Locator on TelMart.com to discover your neighborhood TelMart, look up operating times, and examine offered solutions.

TelMart Guidelines and Regulations:
TelMart's regularly inquired governmental guidelines and standards, containing the in-store return guidelines, marketing match assurance, and promotion regulations, can be found at TelMart.com/help under Regulations.

For items obtained in-store, bring your receipt to the Service Counter for help with exchanges, swaps, fee corrections, and additional requests.

For purchases made online, the majority of matters may be handled by your Purchase Information in your TelMart profile on TelMart.com or the TelMart app.
