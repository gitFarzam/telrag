TelMart Substitute Items

In case a product you purchased is unavailable, TelMart will propose a comparable product as a backup. You get the ability to get, turn down, or return exchanged products. TelMart will demand you the price of any swapped item(s).

Short-term Authorization Hold:
There will be a brief authorization hold on your compensation approach. This is the sum TelMart authorizes to include weighted goods, replacements, and any bag surcharges in your location.

Swap Notice:
If your purchased product is not offered, TelMart will transmit an communication and propose a comparable product. There is a restricted period to acknowledge. The communication will provide you moment to receive or refuse the replacement(s). If you don't get or decline the product given, TelMart will substitute it.

If you're disappointed with the backup, you may begin a return from Purchase Information on the TelMart application or TelMart.com.

Managing Substitutions Prior to Your Buy:
Prior to delivering your transaction, you may decide which goods you don't desire exchanged. You may additionally select the things you favor as a backup.

In case a good on your buy is out of stock and you selected a substitute, TelMart will swap it with the one you selected. In case the good is not obtainable and you indicated you don't desire a replacement, TelMart will eliminate it from your purchase.

SNAP/EBT Acquisitions and Swaps:
Regarding purchases placed with SNAP/EBT, TelMart will demand you the price of the product based on the expense of the good and the compensation approach.

- If you don't pick a alternate compensation approach but permit substitutions: TelMart will swap with a good that is the identical or lesser price compared to the primary product. In case a substitute option is not offered, the primary product will be eliminated, and TelMart will deliver your SNAP/EBT card.
- In case you pick a alternate compensation approach and TelMart has to swap with a good of greater price: Any difference in price for your swap will be demanded to the alternate compensation approach. Sums demanded to non-SNAP/EBT compensation approaches are subject to selling fee.

If you don't desire the replacement, inform the worker at the outlet, and they'll take it out of your purchase. Drivers are unable to take out the product from your buy and deliver it to the outlet.
