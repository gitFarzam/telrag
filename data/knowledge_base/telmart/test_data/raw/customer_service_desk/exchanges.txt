TelMart Exchange Policy

Items purchased from TelMart stores or TelMart.com can be exchanged within the standard return window. You have 90 days after purchase or upon receipt to exchange most items unless noted in our exceptions.

Items sold and shipped by a Marketplace seller may be exchanged within 30 days.

To exchange an item:
- Bring the item with your receipt to the Customer Service Desk at any TelMart store.
- If you purchased online, you can start an exchange through your Purchase History on TelMart.com or the TelMart app.

Items must be in original condition with original packaging where applicable.

Items purchased from dealers or resellers and not TelMart or TelMart.com directly are not eligible for exchange.

For electronics and wireless phones, shorter exchange windows apply:
- Most Consumer Electronics: 30 days
- Wireless phones: 14 days

Marketplace Major Appliances:
- Exchanges and refunds can occur within the 2-day timeframe of delivery for opened/installed products.
- You should inspect your items before accepting delivery, signing for it, and before opening the item.
- If you notice any damage before accepting and signing, the delivery can be refused at no charge.
- Installed items cannot be returned and customers should defer to the manufacturer warranty.

For additional details on exchanges, visit the TelMart Standard Return Policy or contact Customer Care using the Chat with us button on TelMart.com.
