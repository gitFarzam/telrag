TelMart Pharmacy Prescription Pickup

TelMart Pharmacy offers prescription pickup at thousands of store locations across the United States.

How to Pick Up a Prescription:
- Visit your local TelMart Pharmacy during pharmacy hours.
- Provide your name and date of birth to the pharmacy associate.
- Present your insurance card if applicable.
- Pay any applicable copay or out-of-pocket costs.

Managing Prescriptions Online:
With a digital pharmacy profile, you can request refills, schedule delivery for qualifying prescriptions, print your medication history, schedule health services appointments, and more.

To add pharmacy access and verify your identity online:
1. Sign into your TelMart account on TelMart.com or the TelMart app.
2. Select Services, then select Pharmacy.
3. Verify your 4-digit PIN (or use biometrics, if available).
4. Follow the steps to link and verify your pharmacy account.

Once your identity is verified and your Pharmacy account is linked, you can:
- Select Refill Prescriptions to manage existing refills.
- Select Pharmacy History for a list of prescriptions from the past two years.
- Select Transfer Prescriptions to transfer a new prescription to TelMart Pharmacy.

TelMart Pharmacy Delivery:
- Eligible prescriptions can be delivered as soon as today with no order minimum fees.
- Your Rx is filled by a TelMart pharmacist — same professional service, same low cost.
- When the Rx is ready, add it to your cart.
- You will receive a notification on how to get started.
- Choose how and when to receive your Rx, delivered with your groceries and essentials.

TelMart also offers a $4 Prescription program for select generic medications. Visit the Pharmacy page on TelMart.com for more details.
