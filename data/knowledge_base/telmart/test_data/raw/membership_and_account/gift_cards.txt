TelMart Gift Cards

Types of Gift Cards:
- TelMart Gift Cards (physical and eGift cards)
- TelMart eGift Cards (sent digitally by email)
- Third-party gift cards (e.g., Visa, Mastercard, Apple, Google, PlayStation, Starbucks, etc.) are available for purchase at TelMart but are not TelMart gift cards.

Using a TelMart Gift Card:
- Use a TelMart Gift Card in-store or on TelMart.com.
- For physical cards, scratch off the silver coating on the back to reveal the last four digits of the card and the PIN number.
- For eGift cards, find the PIN in the confirmation email.

Adding a TelMart Gift Card to Your Account:
1. Open the TelMart app or go to TelMart.com.
2. Select Account.
3. Select the Settings gear icon at the top right.
4. Select Wallet.
5. Select Add new payment method.
6. Select Gift Card.
7. Enter the card number, PIN, and optional gift card nickname.
8. Select Save card.

Checking Your Gift Card Balance:
With the 16-digit card number and PIN, check the balance in one of three ways:
1. Check the balance on TelMart.com/help.
2. Call 1-400-880-4080.
3. Go to a checkout at a TelMart store.

If you have a gift card without a PIN, you will need to exchange it for one with a PIN at any TelMart store.

Sending eGift Cards:
- You can send multiple TelMart eGift cards to up to five recipients per transaction.
- If you want to attach a message to the eGift card, the same message will apply to all eGift cards in the transaction.

Restrictions:
- TelMart Cash cannot be redeemed for third-party gift cards or network-branded gift cards (Visa, Mastercard, AMEX gift cards).
- TelMart+ annual membership gift and e-gift cards can no longer be used to purchase a TelMart+ membership.
