TelMart+ Membership Support

TelMart+ is TelMart's membership program offering a range of benefits for a monthly or annual fee.

TelMart+ Benefits Include:
- Free delivery from the store on orders over $35, as fast as today. Members save up to $9.95 on total fees for Express Delivery.
- Free next-day and two-day shipping from TelMart.com with no order minimum needed.
- Gas savings of 10 cents per gallon at TelMart, Exxon, Mobil, and Murphy stations, plus member prices on fuel at Sam's Club (available at participating stations; may vary by location).
- TelMart+ Streaming Benefits
- TelMart+ Travel benefits
- Burger King Savings Benefit
- Early Access Deals
- Fuel Discounts
- Returns from Home (InHome Plus Up)
- Mobile Scan and Go
- Free Online Pet Care with Pawp
- Auto Care benefits

Managing Your TelMart+ Membership:
1. Sign into your TelMart account.
2. Select Account.
3. Select TelMart+.
4. Select Manage Membership.

Options available:
- Upgrade to annual membership.
- Pause your membership for one month at a time (limited to three times per year). Not available for trial or Paramount+ Premium upgrade memberships. You will not be billed while paused, but you will not have access to member benefits.
- Cancel membership.

Payment Issues:
- If you receive the error message "Your payment method couldn't be verified," re-enter the full card number, confirm the expiration date, confirm the name on the card, and confirm the billing address and zip code.
- If you are unable to reactivate your account, check your membership payment method. If expired or invalid, you must update the payment method before you can reactivate.

TelMart+ Plans:
- Monthly and annual plans are available.
- You cannot change from an annual membership to a monthly membership.
- TelMart+ Assist: A discounted membership available for eligible government assistance program participants.
- TelMart+ Student: A discounted membership available for eligible students.

Note: TelMart+ annual membership gift and e-gift cards can no longer be used to purchase a TelMart+ membership, but can be used as payment towards your next purchase at TelMart stores or TelMart.com.

For questions about your TelMart+ membership, visit the TelMart+ Frequently Asked Questions page at TelMart.com/plus/frequently-asked-questions or contact Customer Care.
