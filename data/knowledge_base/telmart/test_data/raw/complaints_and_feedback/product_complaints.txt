TelMart Product Complaints

If you have a complaint about a product purchased from TelMart, the following options are available:

Damaged or Defective Products:
- If an item you received from TelMart.com is damaged or defective, it may be eligible to be returned by mail or returned to any TelMart store.
- Start your return in the TelMart app or online by going to your Purchase History and selecting Start a return.
- The item may qualify for a refund or replacement.

Marketplace Seller Products:
- For items sold by a third-party Marketplace seller, contact the seller directly using the Contact seller link on the item or order page.
- Marketplace sellers are responsible for processing, shipping, returns, and customer service related to their orders.
- If a seller does not resolve your issue, contact TelMart Customer Care for assistance.

Product Complaints (General):
- Visit TelMart.com/help and use the Chat with us button to speak with a Customer Care representative.
- Call 1-400-840-4060.
- Email help@telmart.com.

What to Include in a Product Complaint:
- Your order number or receipt information
- The name or item number of the product
- A description of the issue (e.g., damaged, defective, incorrect item, quality concerns)
- Photos if available

Product Safety Concerns:
- If you believe a product poses a safety risk, contact TelMart Customer Care immediately and report the concern to the U.S. Consumer Product Safety Commission (CPSC) at cpsc.gov if applicable.

For additional information about TelMart's product complaint and return policies, visit the Help Center at TelMart.com/help.
