TelMart Money Orders

TelMart offers money orders at its Customer Service Desks and Money Centers.

What is a Money Order?
Money orders are official documents representing a specific monetary value, similarly to written checks. However, money orders differ from other forms of payment in several ways:
- Money orders are prepaid. You must be able to pay for the full amount you want to include when you go to purchase it.
- Money orders are not a form of credit and do not require a credit score.
- You do not need a bank account to pay for a money order.
- This makes money orders an accessible form of payment for people in a wide variety of financial situations.

How to Purchase a Money Order at TelMart:
1. Visit the Customer Service Desk or Money Center at your local TelMart store.
2. Decide on the dollar amount for the money order.
3. Pay the full face value of the money order plus any applicable fee.
4. Receive your money order.

Money orders are low-fee and secure. TelMart Money Orders offer low fees and lots of security.
