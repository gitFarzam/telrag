All burgers are available with either a single or double patty.

Cheeseburger ingredients: potato bun, high-quality beef patty, two layers of American cheese, pickles,tomato,onion, special sauce
Veggie burger ingredients: gluten-free bun, chickpea-based veggie patty, mushroom ,pickles,tomato,onion, special vegan sauce
